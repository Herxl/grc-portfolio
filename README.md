## 📄 Projects

### 1. Information Security Risk Analysis — ENERVERDE RENOVABLES S.L.
> *ISO/IEC 27001:2022 · MAGERIT v3 · 16 pages*

A complete Information Security Risk Analysis for a fictional Spanish renewable energy company operating solar and wind power plants — a sector I know from direct professional experience managing risks in critical energy infrastructure.

**Document includes:**
- Organizational context and ISMS scope
- Information asset inventory (15 critical assets classified by CIA triad)
- Threat and vulnerability identification — including technical findings from **Nessus** vulnerability scanning
- Risk evaluation matrix (Probability × Impact) with CRITICAL / HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW classification
- Risk Treatment Plan with ISO 27001 Annex A controls, owners and deadlines
- Statement of Applicability (SoA) extract with implementation status
- 12-month security roadmap

**Key risks identified:**
| Risk | Score | Level |
|------|-------|-------|
| Ransomware on critical systems | 20/25 | 🔴 CRITICAL |
| Unsegmented IT/OT flat network | 20/25 | 🔴 CRITICAL |
| Remote VPN access without MFA | 16/25 | 🟠 HIGH |
| Phishing / credential compromise | 16/25 | 🟠 HIGH |
